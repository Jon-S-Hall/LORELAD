About Us

We are in the middle of a language extinction.

More than 7000 languages are actively spoken.

90% are at risk of dying by 2100.

With every language that dies undocumented, so does the culture, knowledge and expressions that it encompasses. In our current age, we have to the tools to help preserve these low-resource languages.

We are LORELAD

Our mission is to help preserve languages and cultures by creating a platform to tell stories.

Language is much more than a tool to communicate. The way we speak helps define who we are. Language holds our culture, our heritage, and our history. And we want the world to hear yours. Coming Soon.
